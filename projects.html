Normalized Human Pose Feature


The temporal alignment of two human action videos is subject to many variations, such as viewpoint and subject anthropometry. We address such variation problems in temporal alignment by finding the invariance among videos, i.e., joint rotations.

Paper Video

PoseTween: Pose-driven Tween Animation


We designed a user interface that allows novice users to easily add vivid visual effects to videos. We model the visual effects as pose-driven tween animation of virtual objects, to both simplify the user authoring process, and achieve precise controls of elements’ movements.

Paper Video



TAVE: Transfer of VFX Between Videos


We proposed a framework to automatically transfer visual effects from a template video to target videos according to the temporal correspondence of the action.

Poster



Anisoplanatic Turbulence Degraded Image Simulation


To simulate degradations (distortions and blurs) in imaging in turbulence, we analyzed the turbulence degraded effects caused by each single eddy. An anisoplanatic degraded image is then rendered by accumulating the degradation effects along the spatial distribution of turbulence eddies.



Thermal Abnormality Detection from Surveillance Video


The change of refractive index caused by overheating produces tiny local distortions when imaging a scene. We build probabilistic models to track such subtle motions in a surveillance video for early fire prevention.

Paper



Turbulence Measurement from Degraded Images


We proposed a vision-based measurement of atmospheric turbulence by retrieving statistics from the distortion and blur in turbulence-degraded images.
